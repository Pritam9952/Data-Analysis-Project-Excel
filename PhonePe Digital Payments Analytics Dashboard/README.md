# 📊 PhonePe Digital Payments Analytics Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](PhonePe%20Digital%20Payments%20Analytics%20Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **PhonePe Digital Payments Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Excel-based business intelligence solution designed to analyze digital payment transactions and user behavior. The dashboard provides valuable insights into transaction performance, payment success rates, service utilization, customer demographics, and transaction amount distribution.

This project demonstrates advanced Excel skills including:

- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Data Modeling & Relationships
- Slicers & Timeline Filters
- KPI Cards
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Business Insights Generation

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to help stakeholders understand:

- Transaction trends throughout the year
- Customer payment behavior
- Most utilized services
- Payment success and failure patterns
- User demographics and spending habits
- Distribution of transaction amounts

The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making for improving digital payment services and customer experience.

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

## 1️⃣ Monthly Transaction Trend

**Chart Type:** Line Chart

Purpose:
- Monitor transaction volume across months
- Identify seasonal transaction patterns

Insight:
- Transaction activity remained relatively stable throughout the year with slight peaks during specific months.

---

## 2️⃣ Monthly Transaction Amount

**Chart Type:** Column Chart

Purpose:
- Analyze monthly transaction value
- Identify high-revenue periods

Insight:
- Certain months generated significantly higher transaction value despite similar transaction counts.

---

## 3️⃣ Service Distribution

**Chart Type:** Doughnut Chart

Purpose:
- Understand customer service preferences

Insight:
- Money Transfer services contributed the largest share of overall transactions.

---

## 4️⃣ Payment Failure Analysis

**Chart Type:** Doughnut Chart

Purpose:
- Identify key reasons behind unsuccessful transactions

Insight:
- Failed transactions represented the largest failure category, followed by server-related issues and incorrect PIN entries.

---

## 5️⃣ Top Services by Amount

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Identify services generating the highest transaction value

Insight:
- Loan and investment-related services contributed significantly to transaction volume and value.

---

## 6️⃣ Age Group Analysis

**Chart Type:** Column Chart

Purpose:
- Analyze customer behavior across age groups

Insight:
- Users aged 21–40 demonstrated the highest transaction activity.

---

## 7️⃣ Amount Category Analysis

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Understand transaction value distribution

Categories:

- ₹0–50
- ₹51–100
- ₹101–500
- ₹501–1000
- ₹1001–5000
- ₹5001–10000
- ₹10000+

- Mid-range transactions dominated the overall transaction volume.

# 📊 Key Insights

- Processed over **300,000 digital payment transactions**.
- Achieved an overall **96% transaction success rate**.
- More than **100,000 active users** utilized the platform.
- Money Transfer emerged as the most frequently used service category.
- Users between **21 and 40 years** were the most active demographic segment.
- Transactions between **₹1001 and ₹5000** represented the largest transaction category.
- Transaction volume remained consistent across the year, indicating stable platform usage.
